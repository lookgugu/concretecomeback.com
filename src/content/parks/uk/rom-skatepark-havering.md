Rom is a living piece of skateboarding history. The pools and snake runs require some experience — particularly the deeper pool sections — but the mellow snake run and outer banking areas are accessible to confident returning skaters. Weekend mornings draw a solid contingent of adult skaters, some of whom have been riding here for 40+ years.

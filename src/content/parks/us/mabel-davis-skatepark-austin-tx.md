Mabel Davis is the quieter, more laid-back cousin of the Shoal Creek park. Its smaller size and mellower terrain make it a better first stop for returning adult skaters who want to rebuild fundamentals without the pressure of a big, busy park. The bowl is manageable, the flatground is smooth, and the whole place has a neighborhood park feel — local, unhurried, and welcoming.

South Austin location means less traffic than the centrally located Shoal Creek park, and the surrounding Mabel Davis District Park gives you plenty of space to warm up before stepping onto concrete.
